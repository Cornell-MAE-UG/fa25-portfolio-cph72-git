## MAE2250 Project: SLF Rotating Bug Trap

This project focuses on designing a rotating trap to lure spotted lanternflies away from grape vines. The goal is to reduce feeding damage to grape plants, protect grape quality, and create a solution that could be scaled for vineyard use.
